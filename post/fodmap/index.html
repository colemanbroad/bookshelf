Using public nutrition data

The US Department of Agriculture’s Agricultural Research Service hosts a fantastic resource for food and nutritional information in its USDA Food Composition Databases.

[https://ndb.nal.usda.gov/ndb/search/list]

Here we can find detailed, comprehensive nutritional information from over 200k foods, including the relative content of the mono- and oligo- saccharides which are important in the FODMAP diet.
